The purpose of the files in this directory is the NLO calculation of the process
	p + p -> t + t~

using the formulae from:

R. K. Ellis and J. C. Sexton,
``Explicit Formulae for Heavy Flavor Production,''
Nucl. Phys. B 282, 642-652 (1987)
and

P.~Nason, S.~Dawson and R.~K.~Ellis,
%``The Total Cross-Section for the Production of Heavy Quarks in Hadronic Collisions,''
Nucl. Phys. B \textbf{303}, 607-633 (1988)
doi:10.1016/0550-3213(88)90422-1
