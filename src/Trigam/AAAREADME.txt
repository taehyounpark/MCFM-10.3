The purpose of the files in this directory is the NLO calculation of the processes
	p + p -> \gamma + \gamma + \gamma
	p + p -> \gamma + \gamma + jet 

J. M. Campbell and C. Williams,
``Triphoton production at hadron colliders,''
Phys. Rev. D 89, no.11, 113001 (2014)
[arXiv:1403.2641 [hep-ph]].\cite{Campbell:2014yka}
