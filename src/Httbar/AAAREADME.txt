The purpose of the files in this directory is the implementation of the matrix elements
for the process p + p -> H + jet using the results from:

R. K. Ellis, I. Hinchliffe, M. Soldate and J. J. van der Bij,
``Higgs Decay to tau+ tau-: A Possible Signature of Intermediate Mass Higgs Bosons at the SSC,''
Nucl. Phys. B 297, 221-243 (1988)
